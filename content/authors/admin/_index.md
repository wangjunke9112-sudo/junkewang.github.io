## Research Overview

Our group focuses on automation-enabled high-efficiency multi-junction photovoltaics and materials discovery. We integrate *functional materials physics*, *wide-bandgap perovskites*, and *robotic fabrication with machine learning* to push the limits of solar conversion efficiency.

We combine automation platforms with interface and optical engineering to enable rapid exploration of stable, high-voltage absorbers and multi-junction stacks.

## Selected Achievements
